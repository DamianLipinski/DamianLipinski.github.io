Share Eat!

Overview:

We have noticed that students often don't have time or skills for cooking meals. We decided to design an app that would solve this problem.

We had few ideas about how to deal with this. In the end, we came up with student food-sharing. In the qualitative interviews, we found out that students often cook too much. This platform would allow posting a notice, that we can give away the food are not able to eat.

It makes it possible to eat a home-made meal near the place of residence, prevents monotony in the kitchen, and reduces the amount of food that is thrown away. Moreover, it is a good way to build a helping each other community among students.
