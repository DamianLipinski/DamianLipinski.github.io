Philharmonic

Overview:

Disclaimer: I have no associations with Szczecin Philharmonic. This project was made for educational purposes

Szczecin Philharmonic is one of the most popular musical institutions in Poland. I have designed an interface that would allow the purchase of tickets via a ticket machine. This ticket machine would reduce the operating costs of ticket offices and would make buying of the tickets faster.
